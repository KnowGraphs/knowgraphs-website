# Winter School 2023

We are happy to announte the Knowledge Graphs Winter School 2023, in Vienna, from the 7th to the 9th of March 2023 (from 9am to 5:30pm each day). This year's Winter School focuses again on content relevant for PhD students of the KnowGraphs project and beyond. On each of the three days, there will be two talks, a professional training and a hands-on activity. We prepared a great schedule with experts from academia and industry. Have a look at the [Winter School](/ws2023/) page for more details.
